Mkahawa Point of Sale

Mkahawa POS is a point of sale system designed to make use of IT infrastructure to improve the business operations in a restaurant setting.

Checkout customer

Provide an interface that can be used to calculate the cost of goods sold, amount tendered and the change to be given. The feature should also record the cashier receiving the cash and the waiter who processed the order.

Track stock and till

A way to monitor goods in stock and the amount of money currently available in the till.

Generate reports

An interface that can be used to report daily, weekly and monthly sales. This feature should allow the user to access data about movement of money and stock in an easy-to-understand format.

A project done with Lodwar in mind
